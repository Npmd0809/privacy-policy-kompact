03

Internal Chat Feature

TaskFlow includes an Internal Team Chat feature designed for secure communication within your workspace. The following practices apply specifically to chat data:

Message Storage

All messages are stored on our servers hosted on AWS in encrypted form. Messages are associated with your account and workspace, and are accessible to workspace administrators for compliance and moderation purposes.

End-to-End Encryption

Direct messages (1-on-1) between users are encrypted in transit using TLS 1.3. Workspace administrators can configure enhanced encryption settings for sensitive channels.

Message Retention

  • By default, chat messages are retained for the duration of the workspace subscription.
  • Workspace administrators may configure custom retention periods (e.g., 30 days, 90 days, or indefinite).
  • Deleted messages are removed from our servers within 30 days of deletion.
  • Attachments sent in chat are stored separately and inherit the same retention rules.

Notifications

We use push notification tokens to deliver message and mention alerts. Notification content may be displayed on your device's lock screen depending on your iOS/Android notification settings. We do not access notification content after delivery.

Note for workspace members: Your employer (workspace owner) may have access to messages sent within the workspace for compliance, legal, or moderation purposes. Please review your organization's own communication policies.
04

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To create and manage your account, sync tasks across devices, deliver chat messages, and provide all core features of TaskFlow.
  • Notifications: To send push notifications about task assignments, due dates, mentions, and chat messages.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your requests, troubleshoot issues, and improve your experience.
  • Analytics & Improvement: To understand feature usage patterns, diagnose performance issues, and develop new functionality. Analytics data is aggregated and de-identified where possible.
  • Security & Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent unauthorized access, abuse, or other harmful activity.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.
  • Communications: To send product updates, security alerts, and administrative messages. You may opt out of non-essential emails at any time.

We do not use your task content or chat messages to train machine learning models or serve advertising.

Security

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3.
  • Data at rest is encrypted using AES-256.
  • Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel with multi-factor authentication.
  • We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Incident response plans are in place for potential data breaches.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. If you believe your account has been compromised, please contact us immediately at security@kompact.app.

Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data.
  • Data Portability: Export your tasks, projects, and account data in a machine-readable format.
  • Restrict Processing: Request that we limit how we use your data in certain circumstances.
  • Opt-out of Marketing: Unsubscribe from marketing emails at any time via the link in any email or in-app settings.
  • Push Notifications: Disable push notifications at any time via your device's iOS/Android notification settings.

To exercise any of these rights, please visit your in-app Account Settings → Privacy, or contact us at privacy@kompact.app. We will respond within 30 days.

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA), you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Children's Privacy

TaskFlow is not directed to children under the age of 13 (or under the age of 16 in the European Economic Area). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child without verified parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at privacy@kompact.app.
