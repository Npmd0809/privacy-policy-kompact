Welcome to Kompact! This guide will help you get started with our task management and team collaboration platform.

Creating Your Account

To begin using Kompact, you'll need to create an account. Visit our website and click "Sign Up" to get started.

  • Provide your email address and create a secure password
  • Verify your email address
  • Set up your profile with your name and preferences

Navigating the Interface

Once logged in, you'll see the main dashboard. Here you can create projects, add tasks, and invite team members.

Login Problems

If you're having trouble logging in:

  • Check that your email and password are correct
  • Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies
  • Ensure your browser is up to date

Performance Issues

If Kompact seems slow or unresponsive:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Try using a different browser
  • Clear your browser cache
Pro Tip: For the best experience, use the latest version of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge.

How do I invite team members?

Go to your project settings and click "Invite Members". Enter their email addresses and they'll receive an invitation.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export your projects and tasks in CSV or JSON format from the account settings page.

Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
